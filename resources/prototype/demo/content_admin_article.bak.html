Idega represented in the Baltics

Idega has formed a partnership with BKG Biznesa Konsultantu Grupa in Riga, Latvia. The BKG group is a management consultancy company offering strategy oriented consultancy integrated with a process implementation approach. The company is located in Riga the capital of Latvia. For more information please visit the website www.bkgriga.lv.

The aim of the cooperation is to develop and implement IT solutions for the Baltic market on top of the IdegaWeb ePlatform and standard applications. The IdegaWeb eGov solution will be the focal point and key product.

Idega is now sponsoring the eGovernment session at the Baltic IT&T 2004 conference in Riga. The session will take place Thursday 22. April. At 16:20 Idega will present the powerful IdegaWeb eGov solution and the business concept for the Baltic market. Also a representative from our customer in Sweden, Nacka Municipality, will present a case study from the implementation process in Nacka – a success story from Sweden.

For further information please contact the BKG Biznesa Konsultantu Grupa (www.bkgriga.lv) or Idega Software (www.idega.com).
